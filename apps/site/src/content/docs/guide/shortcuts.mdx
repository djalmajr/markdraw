---
title: "Keyboard Shortcuts"
description: "Everything you need to install, configure, and use Markdraw across desktop and extension workflows."
---

On macOS use <code>Cmd</code>; on Linux and Windows use <code>Ctrl</code>. Press <code>Cmd/Ctrl+/</code> anywhere in the app to open the live shortcuts reference.

    <tr><td><code>Cmd/Ctrl+Shift+O</code></td><td>Go to Heading — jump anywhere in the current document</td></tr>
